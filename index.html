From dinner to verified plate

Three steps.
No noise.

An honest review takes about a minute. It counts because you actually ate the meal — not because you scrolled to "leave a review."

01

Scan the receipt.

Snap a photo as you leave. Cipta pulls every dish, line by line — no typing, no guessing what you ordered, no recall fade three days later.

~ 8 sec
02

Rate every dish.

Star each item. Drop a one-line note if it's worth it. We rate the food, not the restaurant — averaging plates is how truth dies.

~ 40 sec
03

Earn a verified plate.

Stamped, dated, public. Your review enters the record so other diners can find what's worth eating.

forever

Why receipts

Anyone can leave a review.
Almost no one can prove they ate the meal.

Today's review apps let almost anyone rate almost anything — including dishes they never ordered, restaurants they've never been to, and meals AI invented for them.

Cipta starts with a receipt — a timestamp, a table, the dishes you actually paid for. Then it asks one thing: how was each one? Four verification layers, no opinions about parking or the wallpaper.

Per dish. Per meal. Backed by the bill.
