How it works

Three things. No integration.

From sign-up to your first piece of per-dish feedback, without touching your POS stack.

01 Add the QR

You add the QR.

We send you a Cipta QR image and a 5-minute setup guide for your POS: Toast, Square, Clover, Lightspeed, TouchBistro, Aloha. Upload it once to your receipt template and every printed receipt carries it. A starter kit with table tents and stickers ships free as backup.

02 Diner rates

Diner scans, rates.

After their meal, the diner scans the QR on their receipt. Cipta opens, they tap stars per dish and drop a note if they want. About 30 seconds: no login, no app, no account.

03 You see it

You see it. Now.

An email alert lands in 60 seconds. The GM dashboard shows per-dish averages, comments per item, and exactly which dish is dragging your score, not just a 4.2 overall.

The GM dashboard

Not a 4.2 overall. The dish that's dragging it.

Most feedback tools give you one blended number. Cipta opens to a plain-English briefing and breaks every score down to the plate, so you know what to fix before the next service.
